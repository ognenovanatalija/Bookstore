About Us Page

Bookstore is a Macedonian-based online book seller with a large catalogue offered with free shipping to over 160 countries. The store was founded by Natalija Ognenova in 2021, its motto is to make "Books are a uniquely portable magic" and stands for improving selection, access and affortability of milions of books. This online Bookstore also has stores in different locations all over the world and you can find them all on our webpage. Also you can search an employee who can help you anytime for choosing the PERFECT book and find where is the employee stationed, in what country and in which store. In this Bookstore you can find books from different categories and different languages.

Awards In 2012, it was a finalist for the Retail/Leisure business of the Year Fast Growth Business Award and won two UK Startup Awards, Online Business of the Year and Retailer of the Year. In 2013, it was ranked 5th in the Sunday Times Fast Track 100. In 2009 and 2010, it won Direct Bookselling Company of the year at the Bookseller Industry Awards and the Queen's Award For Enterprise.
